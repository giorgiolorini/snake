Guide

Commands:

Use the arrow keys or wasd keys to move.

Press 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 on the keyboard to increase or decrease the speed.

Press P to pause or resume the game.

Hold down the 'r' key to reset game data (highscore and games)


Rules:

Move your snake inside the box without hitting the edges or eating your tail.

Eat the bugs that spawn in the field to get bigger and get points.

For every bug eaten you get (250 + 50 x SPEED) points.
